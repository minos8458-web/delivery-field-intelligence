# AI Support Program Alignment Notes

This document is a planning aid, not a claim of eligibility or selection.

## Current product-to-evaluation narrative

### AI adoption necessity

Field observations are unstructured, short, context-dependent, and often captured by voice while working. Manual categorization creates friction and reduces capture frequency. AI is useful where natural-language observations must be converted into a stable operational schema.

### Workflow fit

The intended operational loop is:

Capture observation → AI structure draft → driver confirmation → trusted field memory → contextual retrieval → repeated pattern analysis

### Business model improvement

The system converts individual experience that normally disappears into a reusable operational data asset. The long-term hypothesis is that this can support independent delivery workers, small delivery teams, and onboarding of less-experienced workers.

### Measurable validation

The project will track capture friction, correction rate, useful retrieval rate, and concrete operational outcomes rather than using generic 'AI efficiency' claims.

## What must exist before an application is credible

1. Working single-loop MVP
2. Real AI structuring provider behind a stable schema
3. Fixed evaluation set of real anonymized field notes
4. 2–4 weeks of founder field-use logs
5. Evidence of repeated problem categories
6. At least one measured operational improvement
7. Basic privacy and data-minimization policy
8. Concrete budget tied to product validation and technical expansion

## Prohibited narrative shortcuts

- 'We will use ChatGPT for delivery work'
- 'AI will optimize everything'
- Unsupported percentage reduction claims
- Claiming platform integration without permission or an official API
- Describing a deterministic parser as production AI
